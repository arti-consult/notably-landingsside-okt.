# Notably product and integrations
Last updated: 2026-02-16
Canonical URL: https://notably.no/

## Product scope
Notably focuses on AI meeting notes and AI-generated meeting summaries for Norwegian teams.

## Key product points
- Automatic meeting documentation.
- AI support for answering questions from previous meetings.
- Support for follow-up on action items and decisions.

## Integrations shown on site
- Microsoft Teams
- Outlook Calendar
- Google Meet
- Google Calendar

Homepage text also states support for Microsoft Teams, Zoom and Google Meet.
