# Notably overview
Last updated: 2026-02-16
Canonical URL: https://notably.no/

## What it is
Notably is a Norwegian AI meeting assistant for automatic meeting notes and meeting summaries.

## Main value
- Automates mote­referat and mote­notater.
- Helps teams save time spent on manual note-taking.
- Improves follow-up on decisions and action items from meetings.

## Who it is for
- Norwegian teams that run frequent internal or external meetings.
- Companies that need searchable meeting history and faster recap workflows.
